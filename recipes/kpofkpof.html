Kpof Kpof

Description

Kpof Kpof is deep fried flour. It tastes pretty good. It is Nigerian recipe.

How to Make It!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar (Brown or White)
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Put flour in container
  2. Add sugar and extract
  3. Add yeast and mix everything
  4. Cover flour mixture for about 30 minutes until dough rises
  5. Deep fry in oil. Do it in scall round clumps
  6. Let cool, and eat!
